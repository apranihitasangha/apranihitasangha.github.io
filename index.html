About Us

At Apranihita Sangha, we believe in the power of meditation to transform lives. Our mission is to provide a peaceful space where individuals can find balance, tranquility, and inner peace. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to guiding you on your journey to mindfulness and well-being.

Services

  • Mindfulness Meditation: Learn to be present in the moment and cultivate awareness.
  • Guided Meditation: Experience deep relaxation and stress relief through guided sessions.
  • Yoga Nidra: A meditative practice that promotes deep rest and relaxation.
  • Workshops & Retreats: Join our special events to deepen your practice and connect with a community of like-minded individuals.
